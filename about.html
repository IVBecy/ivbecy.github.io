About me



As I said, I do more programming than ever. I have realised that I had to choose a path in life. Since I was not interested in antything else, I took it to a whole other level. For example during the 2020 Covid-19 period, I was coding 9-16 hours a day. Straight from when I got up, to when I went to sleep. Too much I know. But I was always so excited to learn and to do something more, that I did not know yesterday. That is the overall, "feeling" that got me into programming. The feeling that I am happier and more succesful than I was ever in anything else. In a normal school day, I wake up, go to school, get home, do my school stuff, then coding until noon. I hope that this much of commitment will pay of in the form of me getting good grades, jobs and a good life.

So if you would ask me what will I do next...
Wouldn't the answer be obvious?!
