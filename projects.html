Password Cracker

Date:
12 June 2020

Since I have re-designed the whole website back in May 2020, I wanted to do something that was again related to python. It is not hard, to figure out that I am really into hacking, and that I love making tools, that break already working stuff. In this case I wanted to build a Password Cracker tool. The concept is, that you take a file full of hashes, then a password file, full of random words, hash every word in the password file, then compare it to the hash file. If there is any match, then the hash was succesfully "broken". I know, it is not the most advanced stuff, that I could come up, iwth but it seemd like a great idea, and it only took about 4-5 hours.

Slow Loris

Date:
2 May 2020

Slow Loris is a DoS method, which anyone can use even with a low bandwith. It is making numerous connections to a webserver, then sending data every 10 / 15 seconds, just when the webserver would close the connection, so that it would not close. I made 2 versions of this script. One you can use with your own IP, but that is not, recommended. The second script, is using TOR Proxies, so your IP won't be visible. Both of the scripts are written using Python.
